**Air Quality Monitoring Dashboard** (4 Major cities-India) 

**Objective:** To visualise and compare 3 air quality parameters across four major Indian cities **(PM 2.5, PM 10, NO2- Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Kolkata)** with threshold-based interpretation and a map-based spatial overview. 

**Tools used:** Python, Streamlit, Pandas, Matplotlib, SQLite, Folium.

**Live Dashboard** https://time-series-air-quality-stcvbuye7gnh28ak33mu7o.streamlit.app/

**Data Source:** https://openaq.org/

**Data Limitations:**
- OpenAQ aggregates data from multiple monitoring agencies; data quality, calibration, and temporal resolution may vary by location.
- The dashboard includes a limited number of cities and representative monitoring coordinates.
- Data availability may include gaps or delays depending on the reporting station.
- The visualizations are intended for educational and exploratory purposes, not for regulatory or health-critical decision-making.
